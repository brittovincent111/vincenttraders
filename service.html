Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

What types of glass hardware do you supply?
We supply railings, balustrades, glass door systems, shower enclosures, partitions, patch fittings, clamps, handles, floor springs, and related accessories for architectural glass.
Do you handle projects across Kerala?
Yes. We primarily serve clients across Kerala and support both residential and commercial projects.
Can you provide custom sizing and finishes?
Yes. We offer customized sizes, finishes, and configurations to match project requirements.
Do you assist with installation guidance?
We provide technical guidance, product selection support, and installation recommendations.
What materials do you use?
We use premium, corrosion-resistant materials with quality finishes suitable for long-term use.
What is the typical lead time?
Lead time depends on product type and customization. Contact us with your requirements for an accurate timeline.
Do you offer warranty or after-sales support?
Yes. We provide product support and guidance for maintenance to ensure long-term performance.
Is your hardware compatible with toughened glass?
Yes. Our fittings are designed for use with toughened glass and common architectural specifications.
Can you help with site measurement or design inputs?
We can review site requirements and provide recommendations to ensure the right hardware selection.
How can I get a quote?
Share your project details or drawings with us, and we will provide a tailored quotation.
